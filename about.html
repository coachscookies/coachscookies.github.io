The Company

This company is the result of a famous cookie recipe that became popular in different areas of Broward County. The creator of this recipe is a man named Dwight Garner who was a coach throughout the Broward county community, famous for the wide straw hat he would wear during games. He simply made a hobby out of baking these cookies for his family and friends. But what began as a hobby soon turned into requests from friends and acquaintances advocating the selling of these cookies. We’re just simply making your wish our command! It’s a family company down to its core with every single member involved in some aspect. We consider it a privilege to make a positive contribution to the community in the form of decadent chocolate chip cookies. Allow us to extend our family and make you a part of it!
